What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that aims to create systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and language translation.

Pros and Cons of AI

AI has both pros and cons. Some pros include:

  • Increased efficiency
  • Automation of repetitive tasks
  • Enhanced decision-making capabilities

However, it also poses challenges such as:

  • Unemployment/job displacement
  • Ethical concerns
  • Becoming biased in AI algorithms
