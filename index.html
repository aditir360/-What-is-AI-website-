What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that aims to create systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and language translation.

Examples of AI in the real world

AI is used in various real-world applications, such as virtual assistants like Siri and Alexa, recommendation systems on platforms like Netflix and Amazon, autonomous vehicles, fraud detection in banking, and personalized marketing strategies.

Pros and Cons of AI

AI offers numerous benefits, including increased efficiency, automation of repetitive tasks, and enhanced decision-making capabilities. However, it also poses challenges such as job displacement, ethical concerns, and the potential for biases in AI algorithms.
